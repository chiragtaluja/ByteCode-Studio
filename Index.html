Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical web development project take?

The timeline for a web development project varies based on complexity and features. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality can take 8-12 weeks or longer. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website or application continues to perform optimally. Our support plans include regular updates, security monitoring, and technical assistance.

Absolutely! We can assist with domain registration, hosting setup, and ongoing management. We partner with reliable hosting providers to ensure your website has optimal performance and uptime.
